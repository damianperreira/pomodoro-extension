Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who do deep work. No bloat, no accounts, no ads. Just focus.

Sidebar Timer
Lives in Chrome's side panel — always accessible alongside whatever you're working on. No tab-switching, no popups that disappear.
Task Tracking
Add tasks, mark the active one, and watch your progress accumulate. The active task is always pinned to the top so you stay intentional.
Live Internet Radio
Stream thousands of stations from Radio Browser — Ambient, Lo-fi, Classical, Jazz, Chill, Nature — with one click. SomaFM, ABC Lounge, and more.
Ambient Sounds
Curated focus sounds — rain, nature, white noise, and more. Toggle between sounds and radio with a single tap.
Daily Stats
Track Pomodoros completed and total focus time each day. Stats sync across your Chrome devices automatically and reset each morning.
Private by Design
No accounts, no signups, no ads. Your tasks and settings stay in your browser via Chrome's built-in storage. Nothing leaves your machine.

The only Pomodoro timer
with radio built in.

Most focus timers leave background sound as a separate tab. This one bundles thousands of live radio stations directly into the same panel as your timer and tasks.
